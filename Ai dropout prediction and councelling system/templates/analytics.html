Attendance vs Risk

Students with lower attendance are more likely to enter the high-risk category. Regular participation is a strong signal of engagement.

Backlogs vs Risk

Backlogs are closely linked with higher dropout risk. Clearing failed subjects early can reduce the risk profile significantly.

Study Hours vs Risk

Key Observations

  • High risk students tend to have low attendance and more backlogs than the average student.
  • Medium risk students often show inconsistent study hours and may need support to stay on track.
  • Low risk students usually maintain good attendance and steady study habits.
  • Tracking these patterns regularly helps prioritize counselling for students who need it most.
