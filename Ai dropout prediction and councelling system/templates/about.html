About DropoutAI

DropoutAI is an innovative platform that uses artificial intelligence to predict student dropout risk and deliver timely counselling support.

What we do

DropoutAI analyzes attendance, academic performance, study hours, and backlog data to create proactive, actionable risk insights for students and educators.

Our mission is to empower learners and school administrators to prevent dropout events through smarter intervention and better decision-making.

Features:

  • AI-powered risk prediction
  • Visual dashboards and analytics
  • Personalized counselling recommendations
  • Educational resources and tips
  • Role-based access for admins and normal users

Contact us at support@dropoutai.com for more information.
